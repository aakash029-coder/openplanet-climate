# Privacy Policy - OpenPlanet
- **No PII Collection:** We do not collect or store any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, emails, or phone numbers.
- **Data Usage:** Location coordinates (Latitude/Longitude) are used solely for real-time climate risk processing and are not linked to individual users.
- **Cookies & Tracking:** We do not use any tracking cookies or third-party analytics.
- **Compliance:** We follow the principles of data minimization in accordance with global privacy standards.
